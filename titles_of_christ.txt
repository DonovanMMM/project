**2000+** Names, Titles, Short Descriptions, Symbols, and all other References of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. Most of the names have been taken from The Words of Prophets, such as Conference Talks,

Published Books, or the Standard Works of the church. Many names have also been taken from other works inside of Gospel Library, such as The Saints, Jesus the Christ, The Living Christ, Children's Songbook, and the Hymnals. All names have citations of where they were found first as well as other helpful information.
